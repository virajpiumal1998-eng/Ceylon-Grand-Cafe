Our Story

Authentic Sri Lankan and Asian fusion cuisine made fresh with bold flavors. At Ceylon Grand Cafe, we bring the vibrant tastes of Ceylon to every plate, combining traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques.

Located in the heart of Ulapane, we've been serving our community since 2023, becoming a beloved destination for food lovers seeking quality, flavor, and an unforgettable dining experience.
